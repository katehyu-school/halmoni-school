About the School · 학교 소개
Hangeul Quest is an online Korean language school built around real-life stories and scenes. Instead of starting with grammar rules, each lesson begins with a real situation — ordering food, visiting a friend, exploring a market — and builds vocabulary and grammar naturally from there.
한국어를 문법부터 시작하지 않고, 실제 생활 장면에서 출발해 어휘와 문법을 자연스럽게 익히는 온라인 한국어 학교입니다.
Hangeul Quest is a self-guided app — there are no live sessions to attend. But every episode, quiz, and grammar note was created by Haeok (Kate) — a Yonsei University graduate and certified Korean language instructor (Certificate of Completion, Institute for Training Korean Language Instructors, Yonsei University Institute of Language Research and Education, 2022). The curriculum is 100% original, distilled from years of real classroom teaching experience.
Hangeul Quest는 정해진 라이브 수업 없이 스스로 학습하는 앱입니다. 다만 모든 에피소드와 문법 노트는 Haeok(Kate) 선생님이 직접 만들었습니다. 연세대학교 학부 졸업, 연세대학교 언어연구교육원 한국어교사연수소 수료(2022). 다년간의 실제 수업 경험이 콘텐츠에 그대로 녹아 있습니다.

How It Works · 이용 방법
Hangeul Quest is a self-paced web app — no live video sessions to attend. Log in from your browser (no downloads or installs needed) and work through episodes whenever suits you: explore the scene, learn vocabulary and grammar, then practice with quizzes and shadowing exercises.
Hangeul Quest는 라이브 화상 수업 없이 스스로 학습하는 웹 앱입니다. 별도 설치 없이 브라우저에서 로그인해, 원하는 시간에 장면을 보고 어휘·문법을 익힌 뒤 퀴즈와 쉐도잉으로 연습합니다.
Not for Hangeul Quest — it's entirely self-guided, so there's no scheduled class or cohort to join. (Hangeul Quest Kids does include small, personally-taught live sessions, but that track is currently private — see below.)
Hangeul Quest는 라이브 수업이나 정해진 반이 없는 완전한 셀프 학습입니다. (Hangeul Quest Kids는 소규모 라이브 수업이 있지만, 현재는 비공개로 운영됩니다 — 아래 참고)
There's no fixed schedule. Most episodes take about 20 to 40 minutes to work through, and you can pause and pick up right where you left off.
정해진 시간표는 없습니다. 에피소드 하나에 보통 20~40분 정도 걸리며, 언제든 멈췄다가 이어서 할 수 있습니다.
Hangeul Quest is for ages 13 and up through adults — a self-guided app with episode-style storytelling covering vocabulary, grammar, reading, and listening in depth. It's currently open to new users.

Hangeul Quest Kids is designed for younger learners (roughly ages 6–10), with live, personally-taught sessions and game-based activities. Kids is currently a small private class for families we already know, so it isn't open to new public enrollment at this time.

Not sure which fits, or want to be considered if a Kids spot opens up? Just reach out — we're happy to talk it through.

Curriculum · 커리큘럼
Our curriculum is entirely original — developed by our teacher and refined through real classroom experience. It draws on Korean cultural scenes and everyday situations, not textbook dialogues. The web app (lesson materials, quizzes, audio) is also custom-built.
외부 교재 없이 자체 개발한 커리큘럼을 사용합니다. 실생활 장면과 한국 문화를 기반으로 설계되었습니다.
Yes. Reading and writing Hangeul (the Korean alphabet) is introduced from the very first level. By the end of Level 1, you'll be able to read basic Korean text and write simple sentences.
네. 한글 읽기와 쓰기는 Level 1부터 시작합니다. Level 1을 마치면 기본 문장을 읽고 쓸 수 있습니다.
Our advanced levels are designed with TOPIK (Test of Proficiency in Korean) in mind — covering listening, vocabulary, grammar, and reading skills that align with TOPIK Level 1 and beyond.
상위 레벨은 TOPIK 1급 이상을 목표로 듣기·어휘·문법·읽기를 체계적으로 다룹니다.
